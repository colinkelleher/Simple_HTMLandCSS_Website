Welcome to Corks Pharmacy

Corks Pharmacy offers fast and reliable pharmacy services tailored to the needs of the local community providing not just efficient prescription dispensing, but also a complete range of enhanced services.

Corks Pharmacy are committed to keeping up with the latest healthcare techniques, offering fast and efficient dispensing with convenient collection and delivery services setting us apart as the pharmacy of choice in Bandon. We offer a range of health check services including blood pressure monitoring, diabetes checks and medication reviews in our discrete and private consultation area. We are also exclusive stockists of MooGoo Skincare and Lamaze developmental toys.
